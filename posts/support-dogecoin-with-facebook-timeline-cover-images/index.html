Support Dogecoin with Facebook Timeline Cover Images!

February 21, 2014 One-minute read
Design • Cryptocurrency • Dogecoin

I made a few Dogecoin-themed Facebook timeline cover images during a contest on Reddit last week. I wanted to create more minimalistic images rather than the extremely busy/stylized images other contestants were presenting. I also wanted to steer away from the Comic Sans MS and Doge-speak in favor of a more serious look. The resulting images are displayed below along with a Facebook demo and anyone is welcome to use them on Facebook (or elsewhere) to show their support for Dogecoin. To the Moon!

This cover image is based on the fantastic Jamaican Bobsled + Dogecoin illustration by /u/intisun.
