About

My name is Colin Sullender and I am an optical engineer at Amazon Lab126. I was previously a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Texas in Austin specializing in optical system design and digital image processing for biomedical applications. I earned my doctorate and master’s from the University of Texas in Austin and attended the University of Washington in Seattle for my undergraduate studies. I was born and raised in Homewood, Alabama.

This website serves as a digital portfolio and mostly inactive blog. Learn more about my education, my research, my coding projects, and my websites.
